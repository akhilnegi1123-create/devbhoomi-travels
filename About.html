📖 Our Story
From a Small Camp to Uttarakhand's Most Trusted Travel Brand

DevBhoomi Travels was born from a simple love — three friends who grew up in the Himalayan foothills of Uttarakhand and wanted to share its magic with the world.

What started as a single rafting camp on the Ganges in Rishikesh in 2015 has grown into a full-service travel company offering 200+ curated packages across every corner of Uttarakhand — from the spiritual ghats of Haridwar to the ski slopes of Auli.

Our team of 45+ local experts, guides, naturalists and hospitality professionals are all from Uttarakhand. We don't just show you the mountains — we take you into their soul.

Our Core Values

Authenticity First
Every route, every guide, every stay is hand-picked by our local team. No generic packages — only genuine Uttarakhand experiences curated with love.

Responsible Tourism
We follow strict eco-protocols — zero single-use plastic on treks, carbon offset programs and direct community employment for all operations.

Safety Above All
Certified guides, first-aid kits on every trip, 24/7 emergency helpline and comprehensive travel insurance included in all adventure packages.
