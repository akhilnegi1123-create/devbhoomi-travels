Tour Details

The sacred Himalayan hills of Devbhoomi Uttarakhand beckon the devout and the adventurous alike. Journey through ancient pilgrim trails draped in mist, where snow-capped peaks pierce the heavens and the air carries the fragrance of dhoop and pine. Experience breathtaking trekking routes, pristine glaciers, and the timeless blessing of Kedarnath — one of the twelve sacred Jyotirlingas.

  • Day 1  — Haridwar to Joshimath (overnight stay)
  • Day 2  — Auli Sightseeing & Ropeway
  • Day 3  — Chopta Trek through Rhododendron forests
  • Day 4  — Tungnath Temple & Chandrashila Summit
  • Day 5  — Return Journey to Haridwar
  • Hotel Stay (4 Nights) at hand-picked heritage guesthouses
  • All Meals — Dal, roti & local Kumaoni cuisine
  • Transport — Private vehicle throughout the tour
  • Certified local guide with spiritual knowledge
