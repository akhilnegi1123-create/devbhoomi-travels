Tour Details

Haridwar — literally the Gateway to God — is one of the seven holy cities of Hinduism where the Ganga descends from the Himalayas to the plains. The iconic evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, where thousands of oil lamps float on the sacred river, is among the most soul-stirring spectacles in all of India. This 3-day spiritual immersion includes temple walks, dawn yoga on the ghats, Ayurvedic experiences, and a Rishikesh day trip.

  • Day 1 — Arrive Haridwar, check-in, evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri with floating diyas
  • Day 2 — Dawn yoga session on the ghats, Mansa Devi temple by aerial ropeway, Chandi Devi darshan
  • Day 3 — Day trip to Rishikesh — Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, Beatles Ashram, return & departure
  • 2 nights stay at ghat-side heritage dharamshala or hotel
  • All meals — sattvic vegetarian food throughout
  • Evening Ganga Aarti participation with puja kit & floating diya
  • Morning yoga session on the Ganga ghats (1 hr)
  • Mansa Devi & Chandi Devi ropeway tickets & guide
