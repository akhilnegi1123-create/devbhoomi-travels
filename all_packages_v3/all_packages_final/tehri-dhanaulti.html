Tour Details

Tehri Lake — Asia's largest man-made reservoir — is a breathtaking turquoise gem nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. Experience thrilling water sports like jet skiing, bungee jumping over the lake, and kayaking, then drive up to Dhanaulti for a peaceful retreat in dense Deodar cedar forests with panoramic snow-capped views. This package blends adrenaline and serenity in one perfect getaway.

  • Day 1 — Drive from Haridwar to Tehri Lake (170 km), jet ski and kayaking session
  • Day 2 — Bungee jumping over Tehri Lake, boat ride to Pratapnagar ruins
  • Day 3 — Drive to Dhanaulti (25 km), Eco Park forest walk and campfire
  • Day 4 — Sunrise over Himalayan peaks, return to Haridwar
  • 2 nights hotel in Tehri + 1 night forest cottage in Dhanaulti
  • All water sports (jet ski, kayak, boat ride) at Tehri Lake
  • Bungee jumping over the lake (83m)
  • All meals (breakfast & dinner)
  • Private transport Haridwar–Tehri–Dhanaulti–Haridwar
