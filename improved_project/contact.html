❓ Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
🗓️ How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance. For Char Dham season (May–June), book 2–3 months ahead.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards and EMI options available. 30% advance secures your booking.
❌ What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 15 days before departure. 50% refund within 7–15 days. Full payment within 7 days.
🏥 Is travel insurance included?
All adventure packages include basic travel insurance. We recommend additional medical coverage for high-altitude treks.
