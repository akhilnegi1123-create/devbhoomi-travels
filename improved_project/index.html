❓ Quick Answers
Frequently Asked Questions
What is the best time to visit Uttarakhand? +
Uttarakhand is beautiful year-round. For trekking: March–June and September–November. For skiing at Auli: December–February. For Char Dham pilgrimage: May–June and September–October. Valley of Flowers blooms July–August. Each season offers a unique experience!
Is Uttarakhand safe for solo female travelers? +
Absolutely! Uttarakhand is one of India's safest states. Our packages include female guides on request, group departures for solo travelers, 24/7 helpline support and all our accommodation partners are verified and safe. Thousands of solo women travel with us every year.
What is included in your tour packages? +
All our packages include: accommodation (specified in each package), all meals mentioned, expert local guide, AC transport, entry fees to monuments/parks, adventure equipment where applicable, and 24/7 emergency support. What's NOT included: personal expenses, travel insurance (recommended), tips, and anything mentioned as 'optional'.
Can I customize a tour package? +
Yes! All packages are fully customizable. Tell us your dates, budget, interests, and group size and our travel experts will design a personalized itinerary just for you. Call us at +91 90275 91352 or WhatsApp for a free consultation. No booking fee for enquiries.
What is your cancellation policy? +
We offer free cancellation up to 15 days before departure — no questions asked, 100% refund. Between 7-15 days: 25% cancellation fee. Between 3-7 days: 50% cancellation fee. Within 3 days: non-refundable. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance for peace of mind.
Do you offer helicopter services for Kedarnath? +
Yes! We offer helicopter services for Kedarnath on our premium Char Dham and Kedarnath Yatra packages. Helicopters operate from Phata, Guptkashi or Sirsi helipads. Booking is subject to weather conditions and government approval. We recommend booking at least 2 months in advance during peak season (May-June).
